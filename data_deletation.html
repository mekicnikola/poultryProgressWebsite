Poultry Progress Account and Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your Poultry Progress account and all associated data, you can request this by following the steps below:

Request via Email

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please email us at help.poultryprogress@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your registered email address.
  • Any additional details to verify your identity.

We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving a request for account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • User account information (email and other profile data).
  • Poultry farm management data (including feed consumption, measuring, and production statistics).
  • Any additional data generated by using the Poultry Progress app.

Retention Period

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or operational purposes for up to 30 days after your request has been processed.
