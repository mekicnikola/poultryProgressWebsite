Privacy Policy for Poultry Progress

Welcome to our Poultry Farm Management App. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and any other information you provide during registration, including name and password.
  • Authentication Information: We use third-party authentication providers like Google to enable sign-in, and we may collect profile information, such as your name and email address, from these providers.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with the app to improve the user experience, such as usage statistics and feature preferences.
  • Location Data: If you grant location permissions, we may collect your device’s location to provide weather information relevant to your farm’s location.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to serve ads within the app, which may collect and use information such as your device ID, location, and ad interactions.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the application functionality.
  • Manage user accounts and enable user authentication.
  • Improve the app’s features and user experience.
  • Provide weather information based on your location (if location permissions are granted).
  • Send notifications related to the app’s features and updates.
  • Serve personalized advertisements based on your interests and preferences.

3. Location Data

Our app may request access to your device’s location to provide weather information specific to your farm’s location. This information is not stored or shared with third parties and is only used to display weather updates within the app. You can choose to enable or disable location access through your device’s settings at any time.

4. AdMob and Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use certain information to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device information, such as device type and operating system.
  • Ad interactions, such as ad clicks and views.
  • Location information (if location permissions are granted).

For more information on how AdMob handles your data, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your consent or at your direction.
  • To comply with legal obligations or in response to a legal request.
  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as authentication providers (e.g., Google) and ad networks (e.g., Google AdMob).

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us or using the app’s settings. You can also revoke permissions granted to third-party authentication providers, opt out of personalized ads, or disable location access through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at help.poultryprogress@gmail.com.
